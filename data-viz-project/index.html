How did Maryland vote in #Election2024?


Take a deeper look into the numbers behind the "blue" state


By Thadean Thomas


Vice President Kamala Harris lost the 2024 presidential election due to losing the swing states to Donald Trump. She desprately needed those votes to win so she spent a large portion of her short bid to presidency campaigning in states like Pensylvania, Michigan, and Geogia.

One state she did not spend much time in was Maryland. Typically voting blue, it would not have been a valuable use of her time. According to a report from Capital News Service, out of the last 15 general elections, the state has only voted Republican three times. Data from the Washington Post's live election coverage shows which counties are responsible for the blue dominance.
